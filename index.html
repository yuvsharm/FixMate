Search Worker From Your Locality

A user or citizen can simply search for required service professionals based on his or her geolocation empowered by Google Maps.

The app would automatically suggest the professionals based on Geo coordinates of the user, typically within 5 kms of the range, which can be enhanced further. The user must ensure that Mobile Location service is on.
